What we offer

Lessons built around you

Whether you are nervous behind the wheel or just need to sharpen up before your test, there is a package to match.

Beginner lessons

Start from the very first time you sit behind the wheel. Calm, step-by-step coaching in quiet areas before building up to busier roads.

Automatic lessons

No clutch, no stalling, less to think about. Automatic is ideal if you want to learn faster or find gear changes stressful.

Manual lessons

Full control and a licence that lets you drive any car. We make clutch control click sooner than you would think.

Test-ready intensive

Got a test booked? Concentrated lessons and mock tests to iron out faults and walk in confident.

Refresher lessons

Passed years ago but lost your confidence? Get back on the road with a few relaxed sessions at your pace.

Pass Plus & motorway

Build extra skills after your test, from motorways to night driving, often cutting your first-year insurance.
